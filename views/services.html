ADR / Voiceover Recording
If your production needs it, we can capture new dialogue and ensure perfect synchronization with the screen. Often times the dialog in a project needs a little help... some breath, click and pop removals; some level balancing and some slight EQ touches. Taken as a whole, these incremental steps can really add impact to any video, game, audiobook or mixed media project. Many times it comes down to finding the right ambience to make the elements blend together and feel like they are in the same space. Or perhaps there's too much competition for the same piece of the audio spectrum, and some things need to be equalized to allow them to work together. Even if you're 90% there, its that extra 10% that will put your project over the top!
