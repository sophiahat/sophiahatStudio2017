About Us

Chris Spencer, the founder of SophiaHat and a guitar performance graduate of Berklee College of Music, Chris has been actively playing, teaching and recording since 1987. He has been on the music faculty at Cornish College of the Arts, Pacific Lutheran University and Edmonds Community College. After many of years of playing with recording equipment for personal projects, Chris built and opened the first iteration of SophiaHat around 2000. Since then, he's worked with various clients in a variety of styles of music, sound design and audio post for radio, video games and film. Chris is an alum of the Shoreline Community College Digital Audio program, a certified Pro Tools Operator, and a registered Apple Certified Pro. Outside of the audio/music domain, he holds an MBA from Georgia College.

Although still actively involved in the Seattle jazz community, Chris manages to juggle the life of a working musician with the challenges of being an audio engineer. When Chris is not in the studio or playing around town, he enjoys sailing, biking and playing with his kids.

Jeremy Wesley, Staff Engineer and Producer at SophiaHat has been an active audio engineer and producer for over 16 years. He's covered the gamut of audio production, from producing and recording bands, to doing video post production as well as audiobook production.

Jeremy lived in Los Angeles for six years, and during that time worked as a production sound mixer and boom/sound utility for many film/tv/multimedia productions, mixed music, voiceover, and multimedia, and eventually became the head engineer at a top-notch voice studio in West Hollywood, working with many celebrities and other notable professionals. His audiobook productions have been nominated for Grammys five times, including one Grammy win!

He has recorded and mixed small acoustic groups, rock bands, choir and orchestral groups and everything in between. He has also edited and mixed audio for theatrical films and documentaries, both in stereo and surround formats.

Jeremy is a multi-instrumentalist who plays guitar, banjo and upright bass in a bluegrass trio. He also has played the trumpet, trombone and french horn as a lad. If he's not in the studio, he's probably backpacking, hiking, taking photographs, biking, or snowboarding!
