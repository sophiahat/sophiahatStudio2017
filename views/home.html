Seattle's Recording Studio

SophiaHat Studio is a relaxed recording environment that delivers pro audio at an affordable price. We provide a creative space and the tools for you to capture your sounds as you hear it. Located in the Green Lake/Phinney Ridge neighborhood in north Seattle, SophiaHat is convenient for most folks in town and surrounded by plenty of food and drink options to keep the creative spirit alive.

Our Schtick

Our services include multitrack recording, mixing and mastering for all forms of audio. We work with bands, singer/songwriters, voice over artists, TV & film producers, game & web developers, and practically anyone who needs music and/or sound design. more...
